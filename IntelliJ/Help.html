ONLINE HELP
CREATE A GROUP

To create a new group, you have to click the button (shown in figure 1) on the left side of the application. It then opens a small window, where you should type in the name of the new group. By clicking "Ok" your new group is added to the already existing ones. You can now choose any elements you want from your datapool on the right side of the application into your group by drag and drop.

 
DELETE A GROUP

If you want to delete an existing group, you have to click the trash button (shown in figure 2) on the left side of the application. It then opens a dialog, where you have to click yes to delete the entire group.
 
ADD DATA TO POOL

To add new data to this application you have to click the big plus button on the top of the right side of the application. It opens a filechooser, where you can add any data with ending of .jpg, .mp4 or .pdf. The chosen data now appears in the datapool on the right side of the application. If you for example add an pdf-document, you can find it not only in the tab "All", but also in the "docs" tab. It makes it easier for you to find a file of a given format.
 
DELETE DATA FROM GROUP

If you want to delete an element in an already existing group, you choose the appropriate data in the group and click the trash button on the right side of the group. After a small dialog, where you have to click "Yes", the data got deleted.

 
CHANGE EDITORS

If you want to change an editor or viewer of one of your data (.jpeg, .mp4 or .pdf) you have to go in the menubar --> File --> Settings. It then opens a new window, where you can see the current editors for the individual file endings. By clicking the "Choose..." button you can choose a new editor or viewer for the format. By clicking on the save button, it saves your settings.

 
SEARCH

 If you want to search for an specific data, you may type in the search textfield on the right side next to the button with the small loupe. Click the search button or enter to find your file. Be aware, that you have to type in the name of the file correctly. You don't have to pay attention to lower or upper case.

 
SORT ELEMENTS IN GROUPS

To sort your elements in a specific group, just switch them in the group by drag and drop. By clicking the save button on the right side of the group your order is saved. But don't worry, the order is also automatically saved, when you end this application.

 
CHANGE ATTRIBUTES

If you want to change a group name or give the group a rating or special informations, just click the edit button with the small pencil on the right side (figure xy) of the group. It then opens a new window, where you can set up all the things you want to. Please click save to really back up all your changes. Now you can see the new group name and with a mouse-over on the changed  group, you can also check on the information given to your group.
